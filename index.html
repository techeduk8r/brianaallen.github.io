about

Instructional Designer. eLearning Developer. Visual Media Creator. Technology Enthusiast. Feline Advocate.

I am a life-long learner who understands the power of knowledge. I am constantly seeking to know more, picking up new skills, and engaging in new hobbies.

My goal is to help others gain the skills and knowledge to be successful in personal and professional pursuits.

My career has been within the education and L&D spheres as an educator, trainer, coach, and instructional director.

My personal passion for design and technology, along with my knowlege of how people learn, led me to persue instructional design, eLearning development, and media production.
